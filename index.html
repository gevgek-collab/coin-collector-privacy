Privacy Policy for Coin Collector

Last Updated: November 28, 2024

Introduction

CollectorsApps ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Coin Collector mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information You Provide

When you use our App, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: If you choose to create an account using Firebase Authentication, we collect your email address and authentication credentials
  • Profile Information: Display name and profile picture (if provided via Google Sign-In)
  • Coin Collection Data: Information about coins you add to your collection, including images, descriptions, catalog numbers, grades, purchase prices, and other coin-related metadata
  • Documents: Files and documents you attach to your coin records

2. Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, crash reports, and performance data (collected via Firebase Analytics)
  • Local Storage: The App stores your coin collection data locally on your device

3. Biometric Data

If you enable biometric authentication (fingerprint or face recognition):

  • Biometric data is processed locally on your device only
  • We do NOT collect, store, or transmit biometric data to our servers
  • Biometric authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Provide App Functionality: To enable you to create, manage, and view your coin collection
  2. Account Management: To create and manage your user account
  3. Data Synchronization: To sync your collection data across your devices (when logged in)
  4. Improve the App: To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance
  5. Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical support
  6. Security: To protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Your coin collection data is primarily stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • Images and documents are stored in your device's local file system

Cloud Storage (Firebase)

  • When you create an account, your email and authentication data are stored securely in Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase services are provided by Google and comply with industry-standard security practices

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure authentication using Firebase
  • Optional PIN and biometric authentication for app access
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability assessments

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Firebase (Google LLC)

  • Services Used: Authentication, Cloud Firestore (if enabled)
  • Purpose: User authentication and data synchronization
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Exchange Rates API

  • Service: exchangerate-api.com
  • Purpose: Currency conversion for coin values
  • Data Shared: No personal data; only currency codes
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.exchangerate-api.com/terms

AI-Powered Coin Import (Optional)

  • Service: Custom backend service for web scraping
  • Purpose: Extract coin information from URLs you provide
  • Data Shared: Only the URLs you explicitly provide
  • Data Retention: URLs and extracted data are processed in real-time and not permanently stored

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  1. With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information
  2. Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers (like Firebase) who assist in operating our App
  3. Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
  4. Protect Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of CollectorsApps, our users, or others
  5. Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)

Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

Access and Portability

  • Export Data: You can export your entire coin collection to CSV, Excel, or PDF format at any time via Settings → Export Data
  • View Data: All your data is accessible within the App

Correction and Deletion

  • Edit Data: You can edit or delete any coin records, images, or documents directly in the App
  • Delete Account: You can delete your account and all associated cloud data via Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Local Data: Deleting the App will remove all locally stored data from your device

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Upon account deletion, your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days
  • Local Data: Remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data
