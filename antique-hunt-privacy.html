Privacy Policy

Antique Hunt - Track Your Treasure Finds
Last Updated: December 6, 2024

Introduction

Antique Hunt ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Purchase Records: Details about antiques and collectibles you track (names, descriptions, prices, categories, dates)
  • Photos: Images you capture or select for your collection items
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates and venue information for purchase locations (only when you explicitly add a purchase)
  • Notes: Any text notes or descriptions you add to your items

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information for crash reporting and app performance monitoring
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about app features usage to improve the application

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for:

  • Displaying and managing your antique collection
  • Providing backup and sync functionality through Google Drive
  • Generating statistics and reports about your collection
  • Improving app performance and user experience

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All your collection data is stored locally on your device
  • Photos are stored in your device's local storage
  • No data is sent to our servers or third-party servers (except Google Drive for optional backup)

Cloud Backup (Optional)

  • You can optionally enable Google Drive backup
  • When enabled, your data is synced to your personal Google Drive account
  • Only you have access to this data through your Google account
  • We do not have access to your Google Drive or your backed-up data
  • Data is encrypted during transmission using Google's security protocols

Third-Party Services

Google Services

We use the following Google services:

  • Google Drive API: For optional cloud backup (requires your explicit permission)
  • Google Sheets API: For optional data export to your personal Google Sheets
  • Google Sign-In: For authenticating access to your Google Drive
  • Google Maps/Location Services: For capturing purchase locations (when you add a purchase)

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Firebase (Google Analytics for Firebase)

  • We may use Firebase Analytics for anonymous usage statistics
  • No personally identifiable information is collected
  • You can opt out of analytics in the app settings

Permissions Required

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To take photos of your antiques
  • Storage/Photos: To save and access photos of your collection
  • Location: To record where you purchased items (only when adding a purchase)
  • Internet: To sync with Google Drive and access Google Sheets
  • Google Drive Access: To backup your data (optional, requires your consent)

All permissions are requested only when needed and with clear explanations.

Data Sharing

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Access your personal collection data
  • Track you across other apps or websites

Your collection data is yours alone.

Data Retention

  • Data remains on your device until you delete it
  • Cloud backup data remains in your Google Drive until you delete it
  • Uninstalling the app will remove local data from your device
  • To delete cloud backup data, remove it from your Google Drive

Children's Privacy

Antique Hunt does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for adult collectors and antique enthusiasts.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data within the app
  • Export your data to CSV/Excel format
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Disable cloud sync
  • Revoke Google Drive access
  • Request deletion of any data

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Local data encryption on your device
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all network requests
  • Google's OAuth 2.0 for authentication
  • No storage of passwords (Google handles authentication)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Showing a notification in the app (for material changes)
  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page

International Users

Antique Hunt is available worldwide. If you use the app outside Bulgaria:

  • Your data remains on your device and your personal Google Drive
  • Data transmission follows Google's international data transfer policies
  • No cross-border data transfers occur (except through Google's infrastructure)

Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) for EU users
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) for California users
  • Google Play Store Developer Policy
  • Other applicable privacy laws

Your Consent

By using Antique Hunt, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

Summary: Your antique collection data is stored on your device and optionally in your personal Google Drive. We don't access, share, or sell your data. You have complete control over your information.
